What is a PUSH?

A push is a force that moves things AWAY from you! Like kicking a soccer ball or closing a drawer.

🧲

What is a PULL?

A pull is a force that brings things CLOSER to you! Like opening a fridge door or playing tug-of-war.

🛹

The "Lazy" Rule!

Things are lazy! An object sitting still wants to stay still, and a moving object wants to keep moving... unless a force pushes or pulls it! (Newton's 1st Law)
